# Dead by Daylight - Crossovers & Licensed Content

## Overview

Dead by Daylight has become renowned for its extensive crossover content, bringing iconic characters from major horror franchises into the game's universe. These collaborations have significantly expanded the game's roster and appeal while maintaining the core gameplay experience.

## Major Licensed Collaborations

### Resident Evil Chapter

**Release Date:** June 2021

**Survivors Added:**
- **Leon S. Kennedy**
  - **Background:** Rookie police officer from Raccoon City
  - **Unique Perks:**
    - **Bite the Bullet**: Heal silently without alerting the Killer
    - **Flashbang**: Create an explosive distraction
    - **Rookie Spirit**: See generator regression
  - **Playstyle:** Tactical support and distraction specialist

- **Jill Valentine**
  - **Background:** S.T.A.R.S. member and bioterrorism expert
  - **Unique Perks:**
    - **Counterforce**: Faster totem cleansing
    - **Resurgence**: Heal when unhooked
    - **Blast Mine**: Trap generators to stun Killers
  - **Playstyle:** Objective specialist and trap setting

**Killer Added:**
- **Nemesis**
  - **Background:** Bio-organic weapon created to eliminate S.T.A.R.S.
  - **Power: T-Virus**
    - Tentacle strike through walls
    - Zombie AI companions that patrol the map
    - Infection mechanic that weakens Survivors
  - **Unique Perks:**
    - **Lethal Pursuer**: Extended aura reading at match start
    - **Hysteria**: Injured Survivors become oblivious
    - **Eruption**: Generator explosion when hooking Survivor

**Map Added:**
- **Raccoon City Police Department**
  - Iconic location from Resident Evil 2
  - Indoor map with multiple floors and rooms
  - Biohazard laboratory sections
  - Classic survival horror atmosphere

### Silent Hill Chapter

**Release Date:** June 2020

**Survivor Added:**
- **Cheryl Mason**
  - **Background:** Protagonist from Silent Hill series
  - **Unique Perks:**
    - **Soul Guard**: Endurance when healed from dying state
    - **Blood Pact**: Speed boost when healing Obsession
    - **Repressed Alliance**: Block generator temporarily
  - **Playstyle:** Defensive and protective

**Killer Added:**
- **Pyramid Head**
  - **Background:** Executioner from Silent Hill
  - **Power: Rites of Judgment**
    - Trail of Torment that damages Survivors
    - Cage of Atonement for instant hooking
    - Punishment of the Damned ranged attack
  - **Unique Perks:**
    - **Forced Penance**: Broken status for protection hits
    - **Trail of Torment**: Undetectable after kicking generator
    - **Deathbound**: Oblivious status for distant healers

**Map Added:**
- **Midwich Elementary School**
  - Iconic location from Silent Hill
  - Indoor school environment
  - Otherworldly transformation elements
  - Psychological horror atmosphere

### Halloween Chapter

**Release Date:** October 2016

**Survivor Added:**
- **Laurie Strode**
  - **Background:** Final girl from Halloween franchise
  - **Unique Perks:**
    - **Sole Survivor**: Reduced Killer detection as teammates die
    - **Object of Obsession**: See Killer aura but they see you
    - **Decisive Strike**: Skill check to escape Killer's grasp
  - **Playstyle:** Obsession management and survival

**Killer Added:**
- **Michael Myers (The Shape)**
  - **Background:** Embodiment of pure evil from Halloween
  - **Power: Evil Within**
    - Stalk Survivors to build Evil Within tiers
    - Tier III provides insta-down and reduced terror radius
    - Tombstone add-ons allow mori kills
  - **Unique Perks:**
    - **Save the Best for Last**: Faster attack recovery on non-Obsession
    - **Play With Your Food**: Speed boost when stalking Obsession
    - **Dying Light**: Slowdown when Obsession is dead
