# Dead By Daylight - Perk Analysis & Meta Builds

## Survivor Perk Analysis

### Exhaustion Perks

#### Dead Hard
**Effect**: Press active ability button to dash forward and avoid damage
**Usage**: Timing-based escape from hits
**Meta Status**: High (despite nerfs)
**Counters**: Killer prediction, delayed swings

#### Sprint Burst
**Effect**: Sprint at 150% speed for 3 seconds when starting to run
**Usage**: Early game positioning, chase initiation
**Meta Status**: High
**Counters**: Exhaustion management, forced walking

#### Lithe
**Effect**: Sprint at 150% speed for 3 seconds after fast vaulting
**Usage**: Window/pallet escapes, extending chases
**Meta Status**: Medium-High
**Counters**: Avoiding vaults, exhaustion tracking

#### Balanced Landing
**Effect**: Reduced stagger from falls, sprint burst after falling
**Usage**: Map-specific advantage, high ground escapes
**Meta Status**: Medium
**Counters**: Map selection, avoiding high areas

### Information Perks

#### Kindred
**Effect**: See Killer aura when someone is hooked, see Survivor auras when you're hooked
**Usage**: Team coordination, anti-camping
**Meta Status**: High
**Synergy**: Bond, Empathy

#### Bond
**Effect**: See aura of other Survivors within 36 meters
**Usage**: Team coordination, avoiding 3-gen
**Meta Status**: High
**Synergy**: Prove Thyself, Leader

#### Windows of Opportunity
**Effect**: See aura of pallets and windows
**Usage**: Chase planning, loop identification
**Meta Status**: Medium-High
**Synergy**: Any chase-focused build

#### Spine Chill
**Effect**: Visual notification when Killer looks in your direction
**Usage**: Stealth, early warning
**Meta Status**: Medium
**Counters**: Stealth Killers, reduced terror radius

### Second Chance Perks

#### Decisive Strike
**Effect**: Skill check to escape Killer's grasp after being unhooked
**Usage**: Anti-tunneling, extended chase time
**Meta Status**: Medium
**Counters**: Enduring, waiting out timer

#### Unbreakable
**Effect**: Recover from dying state once per match, faster recovery
**Usage**: Anti-slugging, team recovery
**Meta Status**: Medium
**Synergy**: Soul Guard, No Mither

#### Borrowed Time
**Effect**: Unhooked Survivor gains endurance for 8/10/12 seconds
**Usage**: Safe unhooks, anti-camping
**Meta Status**: High
**Synergy**: We'll Make It, Guardian

#### Off the Record
**Effect**: After being unhooked, gain endurance, no blood trails, and no grunts for 60/70/80 seconds
**Usage**: Extended protection, stealth after hook
**Meta Status**: High
**Counters**: Waiting out timer

### Utility Perks

#### Prove Thyself
**Effect**: Increased repair speed when working with others
**Usage**: Generator rush, team efficiency
**Meta Status**: High
**Synergy**: Bond, Better Together

#### Self-Care
**Effect**: Heal without med-kit at 50% speed
**Usage**: Self-sufficiency, med-kit conservation
**Meta Status**: Low-Medium (due to nerfs)
**Alternatives**: Inner Strength, Circle of Healing

#### Iron Will
**Effect**: 75/100/100% reduction in grunts of pain
**Usage**: Stealth, chase extension
**Meta Status**: Medium-High
**Synergy**: Lightweight, Quick & Quiet

## Killer Perk Analysis

### Generator Regression Perks

#### Pop Goes the Weasel
**Effect**: After hooking, next generator kick regresses 25% of total progress
**Usage**: Targeted regression, hook-based pressure
**Meta Status**: High
**Synergy**: Deadlock, Pain Resonance

#### Scourge Hook: Pain Resonance
**Effect**: When Survivor is hooked on scourge hook, generator with most progress explodes and loses 15% progress
**Usage**: Passive regression, information
**Meta Status**: High
**Synergy**: Dead Man's Switch, Pop Goes the Weasel

#### Ruin
**Effect**: Hex - generators automatically regress when not being repaired
**Usage**: Passive pressure, slowdown
**Meta Status**: Medium (due to totem dependency)
**Synergy**: Undying, Surveillance

#### Corrupt Intervention
**Effect**: Blocks 3 generators farthest from you for 80/100/120 seconds
**Usage**: Early game control, funneling Survivors
**Meta Status**: High
**Synergy**: Any early pressure build

### Chase Perks

#### Spirit Fury
**Effect**: After breaking 4/3/2 pallets, next pallet stun is ignored
**Usage**: Anti-loop, pallet pressure
**Meta Status**: Medium-High
**Synergy**: Enduring, Brutal Strength

#### Bamboozle
**Effect**: Block vault location after vaulting, increased vault speed
**Usage**: Loop denial, chase acceleration
**Meta Status**: High
**Synergy**: Fire Up, Superior Anatomy

#### Enduring
**Effect**: 50/75/75% reduced pallet stun duration
**Usage**: Aggressive play, pallet respect reduction
**Meta Status**: Medium-High
**Synergy**: Spirit Fury, Brutal Strength

#### Save the Best for Last
**Effect**: Gain token for hitting non-obsession, lose tokens for hitting obsession
**Usage**: Attack speed stacking, obsession management
**Meta Status**: High
**Synergy**: Play With Your Food, Nemesis

### Information Perks

#### Barbecue & Chili
**Effect**: See Survivor auras for 4 seconds after hooking
**Usage**: Target selection, map awareness
**Meta Status**: High
**Synergy**: Lethal Pursuer, Discordance

#### Lethal Pursuer
**Effect**: See Survivor auras at match start, extends aura reading duration
**Usage**: Early pressure, information extension
**Meta Status**: High
**Synergy**: Barbecue & Chili, Nowhere to Hide

#### Discordance
**Effect**: Get notification when 2+ Survivors work on same generator
**Usage**: Gen rush detection, team coordination disruption
**Meta Status**: Medium-High
**Synergy**: Tinkerer, Surveillance

#### A Nurse's Calling
**Effect**: See aura of healing Survivors within 20/24/28 meters
**Usage**: Healing interruption, pressure maintenance
**Meta Status**: Medium
**Counters**: Self-Care at distance, Iron Will

### Utility Perks

#### No Way Out
**Effect**: Gain token for each hook, blocks exit gates for 12 seconds per token
**Usage**: Endgame pressure, hook spread reward
**Meta Status**: High
**Synergy**: Blood Warden, Remember Me

#### Blood Warden
**Effect**: After gates are powered, hooking Survivor blocks exits for 30/40/60 seconds
**Usage**: Endgame comeback, surprise factor
**Meta Status**: Medium
**Synergy**: No Way Out, Remember Me

#### Hex: No One Escapes Death
**Effect**: Hex - when gates are powered, gain 4% haste and exposed status on all Survivors
**Usage**: Endgame pressure, last-minute downs
**Meta Status**: Medium (due to totem dependency)
**Counters**: Totem hunting, early exit

## Patch History Impact

### Major Perk Changes
- **Dead Hard Rework**: From invincibility dash to endurance
- **Self-Care Nerf**: Reduced healing speed
- **Spine Chill Rework**: Removed action speed bonus
- **Hex: Ruin Changes**: Multiple iterations and nerfs

### Meta Shifts
- **Gen Regression Evolution**: From Ruin/Undying to Pain Resonance/Deadlock
- **Exhaustion Meta**: Constant presence of exhaustion perks
- **Second Chance Meta**: Decisive Strike, Unbreakable, Borrowed Time
