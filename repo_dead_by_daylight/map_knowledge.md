# Dead By Daylight - Map Knowledge & Environmental Strategies

## Map Categories

### Indoor Maps

#### The Game (Gideon Meat Plant)
**Layout**: Multi-floor industrial facility
**Key Features**:
- Multiple floors with staircases
- Tight corridors and small rooms
- Central meat hook room
- Limited line of sight

**Survivor Strategies**:
- Use tight corners for stealth
- Pre-drop pallets in narrow corridors
- Utilize verticality for escapes
- Watch for Killer ambushes in doorways

**Killer Strategies**:
- Control central areas
- Use narrow corridors to limit escape routes
- Patrol staircases and key rooms
- Ambush at choke points

#### Lery's Memorial Institute
**Layout**: Hospital with many small rooms
**Key Features**:
- Numerous small treatment rooms
- Central operating theater
- Multiple floors with limited access
- Poor visibility due to room density

**Survivor Strategies**:
- Break line of sight frequently
- Use treatment rooms for hiding
- Pre-plan escape routes through rooms
- Utilize window vaults strategically

**Killer Strategies**:
- Patrol treatment room clusters
- Use audio cues to locate Survivors
- Control central operating area
- Cut off escape routes between rooms

#### Raccoon City Police Station (Resident Evil)
**Layout**: Complex police station with multiple wings
**Key Features**:
- Multiple interconnected rooms
- Main hall with upper balcony
- Library and office areas
- Safe rooms with typewriters

**Survivor Strategies**:
- Learn room connections for escapes
- Use safe rooms for temporary safety
- Utilize main hall for visibility
- Pre-plan generator locations

**Killer Strategies**:
- Control main hall and key corridors
- Patrol room clusters
- Use audio cues in echoey halls
- Cut off access to safe rooms

## Environmental Elements

### Pallets

#### Safe Pallets
- **Definition**: Pallets that guarantee safety when dropped
- **Location**: Near strong loops and structures
- **Usage**: Save for critical moments in chase

#### Unsafe Pallets
- **Definition**: Pallets that can be mind-gamed by Killer
- **Location**: Open areas with limited follow-up
- **Usage**: Use early or avoid entirely

#### God Pallets
- **Definition**: Pallets that create extremely safe areas
- **Location**: Key choke points and strong loops
- **Usage**: Save for endgame or critical situations

### Windows

#### Strong Windows
- **Definition**: Windows with long loops and multiple escape options
- **Location**: Jungle gyms and main buildings
- **Usage**: Extend chases significantly

#### Weak Windows
- **Definition**: Windows with limited follow-up options
- **Location**: Isolated areas with no connecting loops
- **Usage**: Use for quick escapes only

#### Vault Speed
- **Fast Vault**: Quick vault with speed boost
- **Medium Vault**: Standard vault speed
- **Slow Vault**: Loud, slow vault that alerts Killer

### Generators

#### Generator Placement Strategy
- **3-Gen Situation**: Three generators close together
- **Spread Strategy**: Completing generators far apart
- **Proximity Awareness**: Knowing which generators are near completed ones

#### Generator Defense
- **Hex Totem Placement**: Strategic totem locations near generators
- **Scourge Hook Usage**: High-value hook positions near generators
- **Patrol Routes**: Efficient generator checking patterns

### Hooks

#### Hook Placement
- **Basement Hooks**: Central hooks with multiple access points
- **Scourge Hooks**: Special hooks with additional effects
- **Dead Zones**: Areas with no nearby hooks

#### Hook Strategy
- **Hook Spread**: Hooking different Survivors in different areas
- **Proxy Camping**: Staying near but not directly camping
- **Hook Rotation**: Strategic hook placement for map control
