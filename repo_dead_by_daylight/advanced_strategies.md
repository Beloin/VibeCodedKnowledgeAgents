# Dead By Daylight - Advanced Strategies & Meta Analysis

## Survivor Advanced Strategies

### 1. Generator Management

#### Optimal Repair Strategy
- **Split Pressure**: Work on different generators simultaneously
- **3-Gen Strategy**: Avoid leaving 3 generators close together
- **Proximity Awareness**: Know which generators are near completed ones

#### Generator Defense
- **Pop Goes the Weasel**: Use after hooking to regress generators
- **Ruin & Undying**: Hex totem strategy for passive regression
- **Surveillance**: Monitor generator progress remotely

### 2. Looping Techniques

#### Basic Loop Patterns
- **Pallet Loops**: Standard window/pallet combinations
- **Jungle Gyms**: Complex structure loops with multiple options
- **TL Walls**: Long wall structures with windows

#### Advanced Movement
- **360s**: Spinning to avoid hits
- **Moonwalking**: Walking backward to hide scratch marks
- **Fake Vaults**: Pretending to vault to bait Killer

#### Mind Games
- **Prediction**: Anticipate Killer movements
- **Line of Sight Breaks**: Use obstacles to disappear
- **Sound Manipulation**: Control audio cues

### 3. Team Coordination

#### Communication Strategies
- **Generator Assignment**: Coordinate who repairs which generators
- **Hook Rescue Timing**: Optimal times for unhooking
- **Killer Location Sharing**: Track Killer movements

#### Role Specialization
- **Repair Specialist**: Focus on generator speed
- **Medic**: Primary healer and unhooker
- **Distraction**: Keep Killer occupied
- **Saboteur**: Hook and trap removal

### 4. Item Management

#### Toolbox Usage
- **Brand New Part**: Instant generator progress
- **Wire Spool**: Increased repair speed
- **Socket Swivels**: Reduced skill check difficulty

#### Med-kit Strategies
- **Ranger Med-kit**: Multiple heals
- **Emergency Med-kit**: Quick self-heals
- **Syringe**: Insta-heal with speed boost

#### Flashlight Techniques
- **Blind Timing**: During pick-up animations
- **Save Flashes**: Rescue teammates from Killer grasp
- **Stun Combos**: Combine with pallet stuns

## Killer Advanced Strategies

### 1. Map Control

#### Generator Patrol
- **3-Gen Strategy**: Force Survivors to complete specific generators
- **Hex Totem Placement**: Strategic totem locations
- **Scourge Hook Usage**: High-value hook positions

#### Pressure Management
- **Hook Rotation**: Spread hooks across the map
- **Proxy Camping**: Staying near but not directly camping
- **Tunnel Vision**: Focusing on one Survivor strategically

### 2. Chase Optimization

#### Path Prediction
- **Cutting Corners**: Shortest path to intercept Survivors
- **Mind Reading**: Predicting Survivor movement patterns
- **Fake-outs**: Pretending to break chase

#### Power Usage
- **Cooldown Management**: Optimal timing for power usage
- **Combo Attacks**: Combining basic attacks with powers
- **Environmental Interaction**: Using powers with map features

### 3. Perk Synergy

#### Meta Builds
- **Gen Regression**: Ruin, Undying, Pop Goes the Weasel, Corrupt Intervention
- **Chase Focus**: Spirit Fury, Enduring, Bamboozle, Brutal Strength
- **Information**: Barbecue & Chili, Discordance, Tinkerer, A Nurse's Calling

#### Counter-Picking
- **Anti-Stealth**: Whispers, Spies from the Shadows
- **Anti-Loop**: Blood Favor, Bamboozle
- **Anti-Heal**: Sloppy Butcher, Thanatophobia

## Meta Analysis

### Current Meta Trends

#### Survivor Meta
- **Exhaustion Perks**: Dead Hard, Sprint Burst, Lithe
- **Information Perks**: Kindred, Bond, Windows of Opportunity
- **Second Chance Perks**: Decisive Strike, Unbreakable

#### Killer Meta
- **Gen Regression**: Pain Resonance, Deadlock, Scourge Hook: Pain Resonance
- **Mobility**: Bamboozle, Fire Up
- **Information**: Barbecue & Chili, Lethal Pursuer

### Counter-Meta Strategies

#### Against Gen Rush
- **Early Pressure**: Strong early game perks
- **Map Control**: Area denial abilities
- **Snowball Potential**: Ability to quickly down multiple Survivors

#### Against Camping/Tunneling
- **Borrowed Time**: Protection for unhooked Survivors
- **Off the Record**: Temporary endurance after unhook
- **Distraction**: Drawing Killer attention away
