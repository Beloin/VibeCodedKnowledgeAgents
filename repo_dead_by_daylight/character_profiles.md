# Dead By Daylight - Character Profiles

## Survivor Profiles

### Original Survivors

#### Dwight Fairfield - The Leader
**Backstory**: Office worker who disappeared during a company retreat
**Unique Perks**:
- **Bond**: See aura of other Survivors within 36 meters
- **Prove Thyself**: Increased repair speed when working with others
- **Leader**: Increased action speed for nearby Survivors
**Playstyle**: Team coordination and support

#### Meg Thomas - The Athlete
**Backstory**: Track star running from personal problems
**Unique Perks**:
- **Quick & Quiet**: Reduced noise when vaulting
- **Sprint Burst**: Sprint at 150% speed for 3 seconds
- **Adrenaline**: Instantly heal one health state when exit gates are powered
**Playstyle**: Aggressive and mobile

#### Claudette Morel - The Medic
**Backstory**: Botany student who got lost in the woods
**Unique Perks**:
- **Empathy**: See injured Survivors' auras
- **Botany Knowledge**: Increased healing speed
- **Self-Care**: Heal without a med-kit at 50% speed
**Playstyle**: Support and healing specialist

#### Jake Park - The Saboteur
**Backstory**: Corporate dropout seeking solitude
**Unique Perks**:
- **Iron Will**: Reduced grunts of pain when injured
- **Calm Spirit**: Prevents crows from being alerted
- **Saboteur**: See hook auras and sabotage faster
**Playstyle**: Stealth and sabotage

### Licensed Survivors

#### Leon S. Kennedy (Resident Evil)
**Backstory**: Raccoon City police officer
**Unique Perks**:
- **Bite the Bullet**: Healing noises are silent
- **Flashbang**: Create flashbangs from item parts
- **Rookie Spirit**: See generator auras after great skill checks

#### Cheryl Mason (Silent Hill)
**Backstory**: Protagonist from Silent Hill series
**Unique Perks**:
- **Soul Guard**: Become immune to being put into dying state by Killer powers
- **Blood Pact**: Increased movement speed when near obsession
- **Repressed Alliance**: Block generator for 30 seconds

#### Laurie Strode (Halloween)
**Backstory**: Michael Myers' original target
**Unique Perks**:
- **Sole Survivor**: Reduced Killer aura reading
- **Object of Obsession**: See Killer's aura when they see yours
- **Decisive Strike**: Skill check to escape Killer's grasp

## Killer Profiles

### Original Killers

#### The Trapper (Evan MacMillan)
**Power**: Bear Traps
- Place traps that injure and trap Survivors
- Can carry multiple traps
- Traps can be disarmed by Survivors
**Add-ons**:
- **Iridescent Stone**: All traps reset automatically
- **Honing Stone**: Survivors in traps become dying
**Counterplay**: Watch for trap placements, disarm carefully

#### The Wraith (Philip Ojomo)
**Power**: Wailing Bell
- Ring bell to become invisible and move faster
- Uncloak with speed boost and terror radius
- Can see Survivor scratch marks while cloaked
**Add-ons**:
- **"The Ghost" - Soot**: Reduced cloaking time
- **"All Seeing" - Blood**: See Survivor auras while cloaked
**Counterplay**: Listen for bell sounds, use stealth

#### The Hillbilly (Max Thompson Jr.)
**Power**: Chainsaw
- Chainsaw sprint for mobility and instant down
- Overheat mechanic prevents spam
- Can curve during sprint
**Add-ons**:
- **Doom Engravings**: Increased chainsaw movement speed
- **Iridescent Brick**: No overheat penalty
**Counterplay**: Avoid open areas, use obstacles

#### The Nurse (Sally Smithson)
**Power**: Spencer's Last Breath
- Teleport through walls and obstacles
- Fatigue after blinking
- Can chain multiple blinks
**Add-ons**:
- **Plaid Flannel**: See blink destination
- **Fragile Wheeze**: Reduced fatigue time
**Counterplay**: Unpredictable movement, break line of sight

### Licensed Killers

#### The Ghost Face (Danny Johnson)
**Power**: Night Shroud
- Crouch and lean to mark Survivors
- Marked Survivors can be instantly downed
- Can stalk from cover
**Add-ons**:
- **"Philly"**: Reduced stalking time
- **"Chewed Pen"**: See marked Survivor auras
**Counterplay**: Break line of sight, reveal Ghost Face

#### The Pig (Amanda Young - Saw)
**Power**: Ambush Dash & Reverse Bear Traps
- Crouch for stealth and ambush attacks
- Place reverse bear traps on downed Survivors
- Traps activate when generators are completed
**Add-ons**:
- **Combat Straps**: Faster crouch/uncrouch
- **Jigsaw's Sketch**: See jigsaw box auras
**Counterplay**: Remove traps quickly, avoid ambushes

#### Nemesis (Resident Evil)
**Power**: T-Virus
- Tentacle Strike to infect Survivors
- Zombie AI companions
- Contamination levels increase power
**Add-ons**:
- **Serotonin Injector**: Increased zombie movement speed
- **Iridescent Umbrella Badge**: Exposed effect on contaminated Survivors
**Counterplay**: Avoid zombies, use vaccines

#### Pyramid Head (Silent Hill)
**Power**: Rites of Judgment
- Create trails that torment Survivors
- Punishment of the Damned ranged attack
- Cage of Atonement for quick sacrifices
**Add-ons**:
- **Obsidian Goblet**: Increased trail duration
- **Wax Doll**: See Survivor auras near trails
**Counterplay**: Avoid trails, dodge ranged attacks

## Character Selection Strategy

### Choosing Survivors
- **Team Composition**: Balance of support, stealth, and repair
- **Perk Synergy**: Combine perks for maximum effectiveness
- **Map Awareness**: Consider character strengths on different maps

### Choosing Killers
- **Playstyle Match**: Aggressive vs strategic vs stealth
- **Map Suitability**: Some Killers excel on specific maps
- **Counter-picking**: Consider Survivor perks and items
