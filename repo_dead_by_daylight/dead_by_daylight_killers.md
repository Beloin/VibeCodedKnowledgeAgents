# Dead by Daylight - Killers Guide

## Overview

Killers are the hunters in Dead by Daylight, tasked with sacrificing all four Survivors to The Entity. Each Killer has unique powers, playstyles, and strategies that create diverse gameplay experiences.

## Killer Role & Objectives

### Primary Objectives
- **Sacrifice Survivors**: Hook Survivors three times each
- **Map Control**: Maintain pressure on generators
- **Chase Management**: Efficiently down Survivors
- **Endgame Prevention**: Stop Survivors from escaping

### Secondary Objectives
- **Generator Defense**: Prevent generator completion
- **Hook Strategy**: Strategic hook placement and timing
- **Power Usage**: Master unique Killer abilities
- **Mind Games**: Outsmart Survivor movements

## Core Killer Mechanics

### Movement & Detection

**Movement Speeds:**
- **Base Movement**: 115% speed (4.6 m/s) for most Killers
- **Slow Killers**: 110% speed (4.4 m/s) - Nurse, Huntress
- **Fast Killers**: 120% speed (4.8 m/s) - Spirit, Legion
- **Special Movement**: Varies by Killer power

**Detection Tools:**
- **Terror Radius**: Audio warning of Killer proximity
- **Red Stain**: Visual indicator of Killer direction
- **Scratch Marks**: Temporary trails from running Survivors
- **Audio Cues**: Generator sounds, injured groans, footsteps

### Attack & Damage System

**Basic Attacks:**
- **Normal Attack**: Standard melee attack
- **Lunge Attack**: Extended reach with wind-up
- **Special Attacks**: Killer-specific power attacks
- **Insta-Down**: Attacks that immediately down Survivors

**Attack Cooldowns:**
- **Successful Hit**: 3-second cooldown
- **Missed Attack**: 2.7-second cooldown
- **Special Attack**: Varies by Killer power

## Notable Killers & Their Powers

### Original Killers

#### The Trapper - Evan MacMillan
**Background:** Wealthy heir turned brutal hunter
**Power: Bear Traps**
- Place traps that injure and trap Survivors
- Traps can be disarmed but require time
- Strategic placement around objectives and loops

**Unique Perks:**
- **Unnerving Presence**: Difficult skill checks
- **Brutal Strength**: Faster pallet breaking
- **Agitation**: Faster movement while carrying Survivors

**Playstyle:** Area denial and trap management

#### The Wraith - Philip Ojomo
**Background:** Junkyard worker with invisibility bell
**Power: Wailing Bell**
- Become invisible with reduced terror radius
- Quick uncloaking for surprise attacks
- Enhanced speed while cloaked

**Unique Perks:**
- **Predator**: Tighter scratch marks
- **Bloodhound**: See blood more clearly
- **Shadowborn**: Wider field of view

**Playstyle:** Stealth and ambush tactics

#### The Hillbilly - Max Thompson Jr.
**Background:** Chainsaw-wielding farm boy
**Power: Chainsaw**
- Chainsaw sprint for instant downs
- Overheat mechanic for balance
- Map mobility and chase ending

**Unique Perks:**
- **Enduring**: Reduced stun duration
- **Lightborn**: Immunity to flashlights
- **Tinkerer**: Undetectable when generator nearly complete

**Playstyle:** High mobility and instant pressure

#### The Nurse - Sally Smithson
**Background:** War nurse with supernatural powers
**Power: Spencer's Last Breath**
- Teleport through walls and objects
- Fatigue after blinking
- Mastery required for effective use

**Unique Perks:**
- **Stridor**: Louder injured sounds
- **Thanatophobia**: Slowdown for injured Survivors
- **A Nurse's Calling**: See healing Survivors

**Playstyle:** High skill ceiling, map ignoring

### Licensed Killers

#### The Ghost Face - Danny Johnson
**Background:** Investigative journalist turned serial killer
**Power: Night Shroud**
- Crouch and stalk Survivors
- Marked Survivors can be insta-downed
- Stealth gameplay with limited detection

**Unique Perks:**
- **I'm All Ears**: See Survivor auras after fast vault
- **Thrilling Tremors**: Block generators after picking up Survivor
- **Furtive Chase**: Reduced terror radius when chasing Obsession

**Playstyle:** Stealth marking and surprise attacks

#### The Pig - Amanda Young
**Background:** Jigsaw's apprentice from Saw franchise
**Power: Ambush Dash**
- Reverse bear traps on downed Survivors
- Dash attack from crouch
- Timer-based pressure mechanics

**Unique Perks:**
- **Hangman's Trick**: See Survivor auras when sabotaging hooks
- **Surveillance**: See generator regression
- **Make Your Choice**: Exposed status for far unhookers

**Playstyle:** Slowdown and timed pressure

#### Nemesis - Resident Evil
**Background:** Bio-organic weapon from Resident Evil
**Power: T-Virus**
- Tentacle strike through walls
- Zombie AI companions
- Infection mechanic for Survivors

**Unique Perks:**
- **Lethal Pursuer**: Extended aura reading at match start
- **Hysteria**: Injured Survivors become oblivious
- **Eruption**: Generator explosion when hooking Survivor

**Playstyle:** Area control and infection pressure

#### Pyramid Head - Silent Hill
**Background:** Executioner from Silent Hill
**Power: Rites of Judgment**
- Trail of Torment that damages Survivors
- Cage of Atonement for instant hooking
- Punishment of the Damned ranged attack

**Unique Perks:**
- **Forced Penance**: Broken status for protection hits
- **Trail of Torment**: Undetectable after kicking generator
- **Deathbound**: Oblivious status for distant healers

**Playstyle:** Ranged attacks and cage mechanics

## Killer Perk Categories

### Generator Defense Perks
**Purpose:** Slow generator progress and create pressure

**Key Perks:**
- **Pop Goes the Weasel**: Generator regression after hooking
- **Ruin**: Automatic generator regression
- **Corrupt Intervention**: Block distant generators early
- **Deadlock**: Block generator after completion

### Chase & Detection Perks
**Purpose:** Improve chase efficiency and Survivor tracking

**Key Perks:**
- **Barbecue & Chilli**: See Survivor auras after hooking
- **I'm All Ears**: See vaulting Survivor auras
- **Spies from the Shadows**: Crow notifications
- **Whispers**: Know when Survivors are nearby

### Information Perks
**Purpose:** Gather intelligence about Survivor locations and actions

**Key Perks:**
- **Discordance**: Generator cooperation notifications
- **Tinkerer**: Undetectable when generator nearly done
- **Bitter Murmur**: See Survivor auras after generator completion
- **Thrilling Tremors**: Generator blocking information

### Slowdown & Pressure Perks
**Purpose:** Create game-wide pressure and slowdown

**Key Perks:**
- **Thanatophobia**: Slowdown for injured Survivors
- **Dying Light**: Slowdown when Obsession is dead
- **No Way Out**: Block exit gates after unhooks
- **Remember Me**: Longer gate opening for Obsession

### Hex Perks
**Purpose:** High-risk, high-reward perks tied to totems

**Key Perks:**
- **Hex: Ruin**: Automatic generator regression
- **Hex: Devour Hope**: Moris and speed after unhooks
- **Hex: No One Escapes Death**: Speed and insta-down endgame
- **Hex: Undying**: Protects other hex totems

## Common Mistakes & How to Avoid Them

### Chase Management Errors
- **Tunnel Vision**: Focusing too long on one Survivor
- **Poor Time Investment**: Wasting time on difficult chases
- **Resource Waste**: Breaking unnecessary pallets
- **Predictable Patterns**: Repeating same chase tactics

### Map Control Mistakes
- **Over-Patrolling**: Moving too much between areas
- **Generator Neglect**: Ignoring generator progress
- **Poor Hook Placement**: Strategic hooking errors
- **Area Over-commitment**: Staying in one area too long

### Power Usage Errors
- **Inefficient Usage**: Wasting power charges
- **Poor Timing**: Using powers at wrong moments
- **Add-on Mismanagement**: Not utilizing add-ons effectively
- **Power Over-reliance**: Depending too much on power
