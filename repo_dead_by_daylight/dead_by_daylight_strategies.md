# Dead by Daylight - Advanced Strategies Guide

## Overview

Dead by Daylight is a game of strategy, psychology, and mechanical skill. Mastering both Survivor and Killer gameplay requires understanding advanced tactics, mind games, and strategic decision-making.

## Survivor Strategies

### Team Coordination & Communication

**Generator Assignment Strategy:**
- **Split Pressure**: Work on different generators simultaneously
- **Three-Gen Prevention**: Avoid clustering final generators
- **Priority Repair**: Focus on central or dangerous generators first
- **Backup Planning**: Have alternative generators ready

**Hook Rescue Coordination:**
- **Safe Unhook Timing**: Wait for Killer to leave area
- **Bait Rescues**: Distract Killer while teammate unhooks
- **Trade Strategy**: Intentional hook trades to spread pressure
- **Endgame Rescues**: High-risk saves when gates are powered

**Information Sharing:**
- **Killer Location**: Constant updates on Killer position
- **Generator Progress**: Share repair status
- **Item Usage**: Coordinate item consumption

## Map-Specific Strategies

**Survivor Strategies:**
- Use narrow corridors for quick escapes
- Leverage multiple floors for vertical movement
- Utilize locker clusters for hiding
- Coordinate generator repairs in central areas

**Killer Strategies:**
- Control choke points and hallways
- Use powers that work well in confined spaces
- Patrol central generator clusters
- Utilize audio cues in echoey environments

**Survivor Strategies:**

- Utilize natural cover and terrain

**Killer Strategies:**
